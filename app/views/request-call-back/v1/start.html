Get help buying for schools

If you need help with procuring goods or services for your school, the Department for Education can offer advice and support.

In some cases, the Department for Education team may be able to manage your procurement for you.

Use this service to:

  • ask for help with your procurement
  • tell the Department for Education team about your situation
  • find other resources that can help

This service is open to:

  • state schools, including academies and multi academy trusts
  • special schools
  • maintained nursery schools
