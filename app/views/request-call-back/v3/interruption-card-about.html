About the supported buying for schools service

This service is intended to help make buying goods or services easier and cheaper for schools.

By filling out this form, you can request help from the Department for Educationâ€™s supported buying team. This team is made up of procurement experts with experience in buying for schools.

The Department for Education (DfE) team can help you by:

  • providing guidance and answering questions you may have about buying for your school or multi-academy trust
  • helping you find a framework for your purchase
  • helping you with legal or compliance issues
  • supporting you with particularly difficult or complex procurements

You can ask for their help by filling out this form.

Other help is available from DfE:

  • you can read advice about how to plan a procurement
  • you may be able to buy what you need more easily using an approved framework
  • if you are buying a catering service, you can follow a step by step process to create a specification
