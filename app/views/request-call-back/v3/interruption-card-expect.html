What happens after you fill in the form

After you fill in the form, your answers will be sent to the supported buying team. A member of the team will review your answers and get in touch with you.

The advice they give may include:

  • an answer to a specific question you have asked
  • a list of frameworks that might meet your needs
  • a recommended buying process that you could follow

For particularly difficult or complex procurements, the supported buying team may be able to assist you throughout the process.

The team aim to respond within 1 week. Their first response will be sent by email. Once you have received your first response by email you will be able to arrange a telephone consultation.
