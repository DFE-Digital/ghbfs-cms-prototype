Hi Susan Williams

Thank you for getting in touch with the Get Help Buying for Schools team, and thank you for using our online service to create your catering specification.

The next step is to decide how you want to buy. You can read descriptions of the different ways to buy on GOV.UK: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/buying-procedures-and-procurement-law-for-schools

We recommend that you consider buying from a framework recommended by DfE as this can be quicker and easier than running your own process.

You can see a list of catering frameworks recommended by DfE in the Find a framework tool at https://buy-for-your-school-staging.herokuapp.com/beta/phase-5/find-a-framework/matching-frameworks. Each framework has its own list of suppliers and has been assessed for compliance with procurement regulations, ease of use, suitability and value for money.

If you haven’t used frameworks before, there’s some useful guidance on the GOV.UK site about frameworks and how to use them at https://buy-for-your-school-staging.herokuapp.com/beta/phase-5/gov/find-the-right-way-to-buy

I hope this helps. If you need any further assistance, please let us know.
